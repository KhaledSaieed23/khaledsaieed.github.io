Privacy Policy

App Name: Automatic Voids Creation

Publisher: NEXORA

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

NEXORA is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Automatic Voids Creation add-in for Autodesk Revit. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

2. Data Collection

The Automatic Voids Creation add-in does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. All operations — including MEP intersection detection, opening creation, and model modification — are executed locally on your machine within the Autodesk Revit environment. No project data, element information, or file contents are ever transmitted outside of Revit.

3. Third-Party Services

This add-in does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

4. Internet Access

The Automatic Voids Creation add-in does not require or use an internet connection. No data is sent to external servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
