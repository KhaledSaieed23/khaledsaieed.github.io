Privacy Policy

App Name: Nexora Automated Shop Drawings

Publisher: NEXORA

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

NEXORA is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Nexora Automated Shop Drawings add-in for Autodesk Revit.

2. Data Collection

This add-in does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All operations are executed locally within Autodesk Revit.

3. Tools Included

  • Isolated Footing Sec - Create section views for selected isolated footings.
  • Footing & Axis DIM - Auto-dimension isolated footings to grid lines.
  • Columns & Walls Sec - Create section views for selected columns and walls.
  • Columns & Axis DIM - Auto-dimension columns and walls to grid lines.

4. Third-Party Services

This add-in does not integrate with any third-party services.

5. Internet Access

This add-in does not require an internet connection.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.
