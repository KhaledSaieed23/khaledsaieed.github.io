Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from users.

The app operates locally within Autodesk Revit and only processes project data available within the user’s Revit environment and selected Excel files.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal or project data. All operations are performed locally on the user's computer.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Data Retention

The app does not store or retain any user data.

User Control and Data Deletion

Users maintain full control over their Revit models and Excel files.
