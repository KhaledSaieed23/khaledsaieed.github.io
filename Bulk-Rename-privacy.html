1. Introduction

Khaled Saieed Studio is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the BIM Bulk Rename Tool add-in for Autodesk Revit. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

2. Data Collection

The BIM Bulk Rename Tool add-in does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. All rename operations are executed locally on your machine within the Autodesk Revit environment. No project data, element names, or file contents are ever transmitted outside of Revit.

3. Third-Party Services

This add-in does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

4. Internet Access

The BIM Bulk Rename Tool add-in does not require or use an internet connection. No data is sent to external servers.
